About Us

Our approach to health since the first day we serve in our hospital; We use diagnostic and treatment methods that have a direct connection with the patient, specializing in technology, and accepted in academic platforms. In this context, we follow and apply all developments and innovations in the field of medicine with our valuable experts and surgical team with international experience.

Our hospital, which accepts health care as a team work, has created an expert medical and administrative staff by investing in people as well as its technological equipment. We provide the care, treatment and hotel services of all our patients with the principle of "Unconditional Patient Satisfaction" by our employees, who are provided with continuous in-service training and professional development.
